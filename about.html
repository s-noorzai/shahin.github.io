My Favorite Books

  1. Clean Code by Robert C. Martin
    A great book about writing clean and readable code.
  2. Atomic Habits by James Clear
    A helpful guide on how small habits lead to big success.
  3. Deep Work by Cal Newport
    Learn how to focus deeply in a world full of distractions.
  4. The Pragmatic Programmer by Andy Hunt and Dave Thomas
    A classic book on software development principles.
